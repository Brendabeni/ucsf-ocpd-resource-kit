# UCSF OCPD Entrepreneurship Resource Kit

A curated, interactive directory of entrepreneurship programs, 
accelerators, incubators, and funding opportunities for UCSF 
PhD students, postdoctoral scholars, and master's students.

## 🌐 Live Site
[View the Resource Kit](https://brendabeni.github.io/ucsf-ocpd-resource-kit)

## About
This resource kit was developed by the UCSF Office of Career & 
Professional Development (OCPD) to connect trainees with 
innovation opportunities across UCSF, Stanford, UC Berkeley, 
and the broader Bay Area biotech and healthtech ecosystem.

## What's Included
- 18 curated programs across 4 categories
- Filter by institution (UCSF, Stanford, UC Berkeley, Bay Area)
- Filter by career stage (1st–2nd Year PhD, Master's, Postdoc, Founder)
- AI & Computational Biology track
- Search by keyword, funding type, or program name

## Categories
- 🏥 UCSF — Internal accelerators, translational programs & education
- 🌲 Stanford — Fellowships, accelerators & grant programs  
- 🐻 UC Berkeley — Accelerators, incubators & pitch competitions
- 🌁 Bay Area — Private accelerators, incubators & ecosystem programs
- 🤖 AI & Computational — Programs for computational biology & AI in biotech
